# Module 20: Cryptography Essentials 🔐

Cryptography isn't just about hiding data; it's about providing the five pillars of information security: **Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability, Authentication, and Non-Repudiation.**

---

## 📌 Topics Covered
1. **Core Concepts:** Encoding vs. Encryption vs. Hashing, Data Compression.
2. **Cipher Types:** Block vs. Stream ciphers.
3. **Key Management:** Symmetric vs. Asymmetric encryption.
4. **Advanced Identity:** Digital Signatures and Certificates.
5. **Practical Labs:** OpenSSL, Steganography, and CyberChef.

---

> **💡 Professional Analysis:**
> Most cryptographic failures in the real world happen due to **bad implementation** (hardcoded keys, using weak algorithms like MD5/DES, or poor key storage), not because the math behind the encryption was broken. Always prioritize standard, modern algorithms (AES-256, RSA-2048, SHA-256).
